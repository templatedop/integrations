## **1. Executive Summary**

The purpose of this document is to define requirements for integrating
Generative AI (GenAI) capabilities into Insurance Management System to
enhance automation, personalization, and operational efficiency across
life insurance processes.

## **2. Project Scope**

This Module will:

- Enable intelligent underwriting, claims processing, and fraud
  detection.

- Provide personalized customer communication and advanced chatbot
  interactions.

- Support agents with portfolio insights and assist in product
  development.

- Generate synthetic datasets for risk simulation and pricing
  optimization.

- Integrate securely with IMS Core, CRM, and external AI services.

## **3. Business Requirements**

  ------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **ID**       **Requirements**
  ------------ -----------------------------------------------------------
  FS_GAI_001   The system shall leverage GenAI to automate underwriting
               and provide accurate risk scoring for new policy
               applications.

  FS_GAI_002   The system shall use GenAI to summarize claim documents and
               assist in faster claims processing.

  FS_GAI_003   The system shall generate personalized, multilingual
               customer communication for policy updates and reminders.

  FS_GAI_004   The system shall enable GenAI-powered chatbots to handle
               FAQs, onboarding, and guided claim submissions.

  FS_GAI_005   The system shall utilize GenAI for fraud detection by
               identifying anomalies in claims and transactions.

  FS_GAI_006   The system shall support product development and pricing
               simulations using synthetic data generated by GenAI.

  FS_GAI_007   The system shall provide AI-driven insights to agents for
               portfolio analysis and cross-sell opportunities.

  FS_GAI_008   The system shall create and maintain internal knowledge
               bases and training content using GenAI.
  ------------------------------------------------------------------------

## **4. Functional Requirements Specification**

### 4.1 Use Case 1: Intelligent Underwriting

- **Actor:** Underwriter

- **Trigger:** New policy application

- **Flow:** AI analyzes applicant data → Generates risk score → Suggests
  premium.

### 4.2 Use Case 2: Automated Claims Processing

- **Actor:** Claims Officer

- **Trigger:** Claim submission

- **Flow:** AI summarizes documents → Flags anomalies → Suggests
  approval/escalation.

### 4.3 Use Case 3: Personalized Communication

- **Actor:** Policyholder

- **Trigger:** Premium due reminder

- **Flow:** AI generates multilingual email/SMS → Sends via Notification
  Engine.

### 4.4 Use Case 4: Advanced Chatbot

- **Actor:** Policyholder

- **Trigger:** Customer asks "How do I submit a claim?"

- **Flow:** AI chatbot provides guided steps → Offers link → Escalates
  if needed.

### 4.5 Use Case 5: Fraud Detection

- **Actor:** Compliance Officer

- **Trigger:** Claim processing

- **Flow:** AI detects anomaly → Generates fraud alert → Logs for audit.

### 4.6 Use Case 6: Product Development

- **Actor:** Product Manager

- **Trigger:** New product design

- **Flow:** AI simulates risk scenarios → Suggests pricing → Generates
  synthetic data.

### 4.7 Use Case 7: Agent Support

- **Actor:** Insurance Agent

- **Trigger:** Customer portfolio review

- **Flow:** AI suggests upsell opportunities → Provides personalized
  scripts.
