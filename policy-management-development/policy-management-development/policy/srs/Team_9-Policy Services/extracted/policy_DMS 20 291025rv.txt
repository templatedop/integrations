02.	Executive Summary :

This document outlines the business requirements to Scan & upload the required Documents in the Document Management System for the usage of Users of Insurance Management System (IMS) to View Retrieve, Download, Print as and when requires. 



03.	Business Objectives :

The IMS project addresses the requirements of Scanning & Uploading the Documents of a Policy by the Insurant or DoP User and when required.

	06.	Functionality Description :

Before processing the Proposals, Service Requests the required Forms, Documents etc must be Scanned & readily available for Upload as Uploading is a part of Workflow.

Proposal : Insurant or DoP Users need to Scan all the required documents & upload into CMS.

Service Request : Insurant or DoP Users need to Scan all the required documents & upload into CMS.

Soft copy of the documents available in DMS shall be downloaded by DoP Users, Insurant as and when required for processing in IMS as per the Workflow.
